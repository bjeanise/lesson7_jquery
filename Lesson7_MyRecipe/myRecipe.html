Quick-N-Healthy BlueBerry Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • 1 package of instant, plain oatmeal
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup blueberries

Instructions

In a microwave safe bowl, mix together the water and oats.

Microwave on high for 1 minute.

Remove oatmeal from microwave.

Stir in blueberries.

Enjoy with a glass of apple juice, rice milk, or water.
